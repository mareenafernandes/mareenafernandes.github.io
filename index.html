Say Hi!

Hello, I am Mareena

I am currently pursuing Information Technology Engineering from Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering located in Bandra West.

I am a self-taught artist and illustrator from Mumbai who enjoys exploring the possibilities of using software to create captivating and engaging content both for my social media pages and for small companies.

I started out as a traditional pencil and paint artist before trying to integrate my creative thoughts with design software like Illustrator and Photoshop. Some of my favorite things to do include making landscape illustrations, adding slightly more complicated scenes every time and exploring different effects to enhance my artwork.

I see my art as a way to free my mind and release my creative side among all the hustle of everyday life. I am also interested in Digital Marketing, an arena where graphic design experience and creativity are highly prized. I have also worked as an intern making creatives for advertising and brand promotion purposes. My goal is to hone my graphic design skills over time not just to create more beautiful and complex artwork but also as a base to build my career in marketing and B2C interaction.
